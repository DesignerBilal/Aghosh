Our Projects

Aghosh Grammar School

Aghosh Grammar School (AGS) delivers a high-quality education that emphasizes academic performance, personal development, and strong moral values. It mixes current teaching methods with classical concepts to help students prepare for future difficulties.

Aghosh Orphan Care Home

Aghosh Orphan Care Homes Lahore is the flagship project of Minhaj Welfare Foundation, founded by Shaykh ul Islam Dr. Muhammad Tahir-ul-Qadri. It was established in 2005 in response to the devastating earthquake that struck northern Pakistan.

Minhaj institute of Qirat and Tehfeez

Minhaj Institute of Qira'at & Tehfeez-ul-Quran is uniquely designed to give students an education that is international, personalized, and creative. We offer a curriculum that focuses on balancing academia, leadership skills, character-building, and self-discovery.
